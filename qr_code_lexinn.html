Types of Neologisms – Poster Resource

Conceptual map representing different types of neologisms based on Smyk Bhattacharjee (2008).

Short description

This figure provides a structured overview of the ways in which neologisms enter a language. It shows the classification of neologisms into phonological, morphological, semantic, loan-based, and coinage-based processes. Subcategories demonstrate mechanisms such as derivation, conversion, compounding, and borrowing. The map is intended as a quick reference for understanding the transitional and dynamic nature of new word formation in natural language.

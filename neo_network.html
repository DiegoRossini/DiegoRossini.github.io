Reddit Neologisms Network – Poster Resource

Network visualization of the top 9 most active subreddits (2015-2024) showing the most frequent neologisms and their connectivity patterns.

How to explore: The interactive nodes in the network are incel, eli5, clickbait, fafo, yeet, and stonks. First, click directly on these NODES in the network above to watch real usage examples on YouTube. Then, click the following links to understand their meanings according to Urban Dictionary:

incel • eli5 • clickbait • fafo • yeet • stonks

Short description

This interactive network visualization presents data from the top 9 most active subreddits in terms of total posts and comments between 2015 and 2024, based on a Reddit corpus scraped during the first 4 months of 2025 using the API. The network displays the most frequent neologisms found in these subreddits, showing both their connectivity and frequency of use. The visualization reveals interesting patterns - for example, "incel" shows high connectivity similar to "eli5", while "moass" appears highly specific to finance-related communities. Selected nodes are interactive and link to real-world examples of these terms being used in context, providing a dynamic way to understand how new language forms emerge and spread through different communities.
